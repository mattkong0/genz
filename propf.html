Vote Yes on Prop F

What Does Your Vote Mean

Voting yes means that you are in favor of establishing the following requirements for campaign advertisement disclaimers and restrictions on campaign contributions:

  • a ban on limited liability companies or partnerships contributing to candidate committees;
  • a ban on contributions to a city supervisor, the mayor, the city attorney, or any candidates for these positions from someone with certain levels of financial interest in any matter concerning zoning, city planning, or land-use changes
  • requirements that specify how advertisements must display funding and what donation threshold requires a disclaimer.
Voting no means that you are against changing the city's campaign contribution restrictions and advertisement disclaimer requirements, thereby leaving the current laws in place.
